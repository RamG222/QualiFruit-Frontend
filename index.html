About

The Fruit Quality Detection and Price Estimation project is a collaborative effort by a group of enthusiastic college students passionate about applying machine learning to solve real-world problems. Our goal is to create a smart and practical system that evaluates the quality of fruits and estimates their price based on various parameters. This project not only enhances our technical skills but also aims to make a meaningful impact on the agricultural and food industry.

This project is a reflection of our hard work and dedication as college students, and we hope it serves as a stepping stone for more impactful innovations in the future.

1. Problem Statement :

Manual quality checking and pricing of fruits can be inconsistent and time-consuming.
There is a lack of standardization and transparency in the current processes, which affects farmers, vendors, and consumers.

2. Objectives :

To create a system that standardizes fruit quality evaluation and ensures fair pricing.
To gain hands-on experience in machine learning and data analysis.

2. Technology Stack :

We’re using tools and technologies like Python, TensorFlow, OpenCV, and market analysis APIs to build our project.

Key features of our project include:

Automated Quality Assessment

By using image processing and ML techniques, our system can analyze attributes like color, texture, size, and shape to determine fruit quality.

Dynamic Price Estimation

We use market trends, quality metrics, and historical data to predict a fair and optimal price for the fruit.

User-Friendly Interface

Our platform allows users to upload fruit images and get instant insights on quality and pricing.

Scalability

The system is designed to adapt to different types of fruits and regional market conditions.
